Stay prepared and protect your family during power outages with real-time alerts and personalized guidance.

This app helps you take proactive steps by providing timely notifications, tailored safety tips, and even tracking your child’s location for peace of mind during emergencies.

Stay informed with customized alerts that provide critical information tailored to your location and circumstances.

Whether you're managing your own household or keeping your family safe, the app offers comprehensive solutions to navigate power outages smoothly.

Real-time Power Outage Alerts

The app provides real-time notifications about potential power outages using the State of Risk (SoR) Machine Learning algorithm. Users receive alerts based on their location and the severity of the predicted outage, allowing them to take proactive measures to mitigate the effects of power loss.

Tracking Kid Location and Sector-specific Alerts

The app features a parent-child account linking function that allows parents to monitor their child's location and receive power outage risk notifications specific to the sector their child is in. This feature helps parents ensure their child's safety during an outage, especially if the child is in a different part of the city.

User-specific Interfaces and Guidance

The app offers tailored interfaces and functionalities for three age groups: children, adults, and seniors. Each demographic receives age-appropriate information, safety tips, and advice, with seniors benefiting from voice commands and larger text, and children engaging with interactive prompts and educational resources.
